Franklin County Park & Campground

Enjoy camping, picnics, and outdoor fun at Franklin County Park! Located at 7178 Blue Creek Road, Brookville, Indiana.

With modern and primitive campsites, rentable shelters, playgrounds, and access to Brookville Lake and Whitewater River, it's the perfect getaway—close to town, but full of nature.
