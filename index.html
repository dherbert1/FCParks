Franklin County Park & Campground

reserve contact

Franklin County Park (FCP) is located in beautiful Brookville, Indiana. FCP is the ideal place to go camping, relax and enjoy Brookville’s summer playground. The park offers both modern camping with electricity and water, and also primitive camping. The park also offers four shelters which can be rented for family reunions, receptions, parties and picnics, just to name a few ideas. Restrooms are available at all shelters as well as playground equipment. While staying at FCP you not only have access to: Brookville Lake, which offers boating, fishing, beaches and swimming; and several canoe liveries on the Whitewater river, you are also centrally located to many historical sites. FCP is conveniently located near churches, stores, restaurants and laundromats. Why not plan a relaxing getaway from home and enjoy the facilities at Franklin County Park?
